Overwhelmed By My Own Feelings

Overwhelmed by my own feelings
overwhelmed by flowers and poetry
sometimes I might be too intense
sometimes I might be too impulsive
nevertheless
I love to love
I love to be in love
I love intense passions
as I’m very passionate
my imagination and emotions run wild
I dream of dreaming
you are always in my mind
my heart is yours
my soul is a crimson rose garden
wherever I am
you are always with me.


                        - Esther Elizabeth Racah
