I love to love you

I love to love you
an infinite number of times
as many as the stars in the universe
I love to love you infinitely and recklessly
I cannot stop loving you
my soul belongs exclusively to you
my body belongs solely to you
it is an overwhelming passion
like an inextinguishable flame
which is burning into my soul
I never stop dreaming of you
my love for you is a shining star.
                


                        - Esther Elizabeth Racah
